**Major Outage (>24 hours):**
- Automatic 1-week service credit
- Plus SLA credits per SLA document
- Or prorated refund if requested

**Extended Outage (>72 hours):**
- Full month refund
- Plus 1-month free service
- Or full refund and account credit

**Our Fault:**
- Full refund of current month
- 3 months free service
- Free premium support for recovery
- Migration assistance if switching providers

**Third-Party Fault:**
- Work with third-party provider
- Assist with recovery

**Our Responsibility:**
- Affected users: Full refund + 6 months free

**Overcharge:**

**Double Charge:**

**Fraudulent Use:**

- No liability for unauthorized use (with prompt reporting)
