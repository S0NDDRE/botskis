# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)
**Mindframe AI - GDPR Article 28**
**For Enterprise Customers**

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between:

**DATA CONTROLLER:** Customer (you)
**DATA PROCESSOR:** Mindframe AI

## 1. DEFINITIONS
- **Personal Data:** As defined in GDPR
- **Processing:** As defined in GDPR
- **Data Subject:** Individuals whose data is processed

## 2. SCOPE OF PROCESSING

**Subject Matter:** Provision of AI automation services

**Duration:** Term of subscription

**Nature and Purpose:**
- AI agent processing of customer data
- Storage and backup
- Support services

**Types of Personal Data:**
- Customer employee data
- Customer end-user data
- Business contact information

**Categories of Data Subjects:**
- Customer employees
- Customer customers/users
- Business contacts

## 3. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS

Mindframe AI shall:
✅ Process data only on documented instructions
✅ Ensure confidentiality of personnel
✅ Implement appropriate security measures
✅ Engage sub-processors only with consent
✅ Assist with data subject requests
✅ Assist with security and breach obligations
✅ Delete or return data upon termination
✅ Make available information for audits

## 4. SECURITY MEASURES

**Technical Measures:**
- Encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256)
- Access controls (RBAC)
- Network security (firewalls, IDS)
- Vulnerability scanning

**Organizational Measures:**
- Security policies
- Employee training
- Incident response plan
- Regular audits

## 5. SUB-PROCESSORS

**Current Sub-Processors:**
- AWS/Azure (hosting - EU region)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- SendGrid (email delivery)

**New Sub-Processors:**
- 30 days advance notice
- Right to object

## 6. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

Mindframe AI will assist Customer in responding to:
- Access requests
- Correction requests
- Deletion requests
- Data portability requests
- Restriction requests
- Objection requests

Response time: Within 30 days

## 7. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

**Obligation:**
Notify Customer within 24 hours of becoming aware of breach

**Notification includes:**
- Nature of breach
- Categories and number of data subjects affected
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken or proposed

## 8. DATA RETURN/DELETION

**Upon Termination:**
Customer may request:
- Export of all data (within 30 days)
- Deletion of all data (within 30 days)

**Format:** JSON, CSV, or database dump

**Certification:** Upon request, certify deletion

## 9. AUDIT RIGHTS

Customer may:
- Request audit reports (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
- Conduct on-site audit (reasonable notice, once per year)
- Engage third-party auditor

**Cost:** Customer bears cost of audits

## 10. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

**Data Location:** EU (primary)

**Transfers outside EU:**
Only with Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequate safeguards

## 11. LIABILITY

**Processor Liability:**
Mindframe AI liable for damages caused by processing in violation of GDPR

**Limitation:**
As per Terms of Service

## 12. TERM AND TERMINATION

**Term:** Duration of subscription

**Survival:** Sections 6, 7, 8, 9 survive termination
