# MINDFRAME - GDPR COMPLIANCE GUIDE

**General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance**

## OVERVIEW

Mindframe is fully GDPR compliant. This document outlines our compliance measures.

## 1. LAWFUL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

### Contract (Art. 6(1)(b))
- Providing the Service
- Payment processing
- Customer support

### Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f))
- Service improvement
- Fraud prevention
- Security

### Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))
- Marketing emails
- Optional features
- Cookies

### Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))
- Tax compliance
- Legal requests

## 2. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

### Right to Access (Art. 15)
**Implementation:**
- Self-service export in settings
- Email request: privacy@mindframe.ai
- Response within 30 days

### Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
**Implementation:**
- Edit profile in settings
- Contact support for corrections

### Right to Erasure (Art. 17)
**Implementation:**
- Delete account in settings
- Complete deletion within 30 days
- Backups deleted within 90 days

### Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
**Implementation:**
- Export data in JSON/CSV format
- Download via settings
- Includes all personal data

### Right to Object (Art. 21)
**Implementation:**
- Opt-out of marketing
- Disable analytics cookies
- Request processing limitation

### Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18)
**Implementation:**
- Temporarily disable account
- Limit data processing
- Maintain data integrity

### Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))
**Implementation:**
- Unsubscribe links in emails
- Settings page toggles
- Immediate effect
